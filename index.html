Fayetteville Industrial Market Overview

The Fayetteville industrial market contains roughly 20.3 million SF of inventory and carries a vacancy rate of 11.3% as of Q2 2026, up 1.9% year-over-year — reflecting the national normalization following the post-pandemic absorption surge. No industrial space is currently under construction in Fayetteville, compared to a 10-year average of 690,000 SF under construction, which will limit future supply additions and support rent stabilization.

The East Fayetteville submarket — home to the subject property — has a vacancy rate of 16.3% as of Q2 2026, with roughly 2.0 million SF of available space and an availability rate of 20.8%. No new supply is under construction in the submarket. Market rents in East Fayetteville average $7.30/SF overall, with logistics buildings at $6.60/SF and rents growing 3.3% year-over-year. Five-year average annual rent growth stands at 8.3%, reflecting strong long-term demand fundamentals.

Fayetteville's economy is anchored by Fort Bragg, the largest U.S. Army installation in the country, providing a stable, recession-resistant demand base for industrial occupiers. Key corporate operators in the market include Amazon (1M SF distribution), The Campbell's Company (625,000 SF), MANN+HUMMEL, Purlator, and Goodyear Tire and Rubber, Inc. NVR opened a 145,000 SF manufacturing facility in the region in 2024. The subject's location within the 465-acre Fayetteville Business Park — alongside Amazon, FedEx, NVR, and Soffe — positions it in the most visible and active industrial campus in the market.

Lease Rate Opinion — Prepared for Austin Wilson / RealtyLink

Based on a review of signed industrial lease comparables in the East Fayetteville submarket and an analysis of active on-market listings across Fayetteville, it is our opinion that 2057 Weeks Rd — Building 1 supports a proposed NNN lease rate of $6.95/SF annually ($31,623/month) as of May 5, 2026.

The subject building is a 54,600 SF reinforced concrete distribution facility delivered in April 2025 — one of the newest industrial buildings in the Fayetteville market. Its 36' clear ceiling height, 14 exterior dock doors, ESFR sprinkler system, and 44 trailer stalls represent a Class A industrial specification that commands a meaningful premium over the existing comp set, which consists predominantly of older-vintage, lower-clear-height product in the $3.15–$5.95/SF NNN range.

The most comparable signed transaction is 103 Tom Starling Rd (125,000 SF, Class A, built 2023, same East Fayetteville submarket), which executed at $0.51/SF/month ($6.12/SF annually) in October 2024. The subject's newer delivery (2025 vs. 2023), 36' clear height, and Fayetteville Business Park address — alongside co-tenants Amazon, FedEx, NVR, and Soffe — support a modest premium over that transaction, yielding the recommended rate of $6.95/SF. This rate also sits above the East Fayetteville logistics market average of $6.60/SF, reflecting the property's superior specification and the absence of competing new Class A supply in the pipeline.
